Mobile App for tracking your gym workouts with key features:

Customizable Workout Routines:

Create and personalize your workout routines based on your fitness goals, whether it's strength training, endurance, or a balanced mix.

Real-time Workout Tracking:

Enable workouts as you start, and seamlessly record your sets and reps for each exercise during your training session.

Data Storage and Profile Management:

Save your workout history and track your training time effortlessly. Your workout data is securely stored in your profile, allowing you to revisit and analyze your progress over time.

Progress Statistics:

The app calculates your maximum volume and one-repetition maximum for each exercise, providing a clear overview of your strength gains.
